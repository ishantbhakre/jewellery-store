Our Heritage

A Legacy Forged
in Silver

Since 1983, Bafna Silver House has been the heartbeat of silver jewellery in Nagpur. What began as a passionate pursuit of purity has blossomed into the city's most trusted wholesale destination for 925 and 999 silver.

Every piece carries four decades of expertise — where traditional craftsmanship meets contemporary elegance. Our artisans pour devotion into each anklet, bracelet, necklace, and ring that leaves our atelier.

We believe fine silver is not merely an accessory — it is a story, a memory, a gift that endures. That is why we hold ourselves to the highest standards of purity, design, and care.
